— Preparations —

Before You Go

Payments

WeChat Pay & Alipay accept foreign Visa/Mastercard via "Tour Card" — set up both before flying. Carry ¥500 cash as backup.

Apps

WeChat, Alipay, DiDi, Amap/Gaode Maps, Universal Beijing Resort app. A VPN is needed for Google and Instagram.

Weather

−5°C to 3°C through December. Thermal layers, waterproof boots, gloves and hand warmers for all six. Wind chill at the Great Wall and Tiananmen can feel like −15°C.

Tickets

Forbidden City + Tiananmen and Temple of Heaven open exactly 7 days ahead. Universal Studios should be booked weeks out. All passports needed at booking.

Transit

Subway is fast and cheap (¥3–7/trip). DiDi XL or MPV suits the full party of six. The hotel concierge can arrange a full-day driver.

Residence

Novotel Peace Beijing, No. 3 Jinyu Hutong, Wangfujing. Six minutes' walk to Dengshikou Metro (Line 5); 0.8km from the Forbidden City's East Gate.

Visas

Singapore passport holders enjoy 144-hour visa-free transit — reconfirm the policy 60 days before travel.

Emergency

Police 110 · Ambulance 120 · Tourist Hotline 12301. Hotel concierge speaks English.

— Field Notes —

Local Tips & What to Avoid

Scams

Rickshaw tours near Tiananmen quote ¥10 but bill ¥100+. A taxi refusing the meter means walk away — DiDi shows the price upfront.

Language

The hotel concierge speaks English. Download an offline Mandarin pack for Google Translate, and ask the hotel to print key addresses in Chinese characters.

Internet

A VPN is required for Google, Instagram and WhatsApp — install before entering China. WeChat works without one for all local communication.

Toilets

Carry pocket tissues — public restrooms often don't provide paper. Squat toilets are common outside hotels and tourist sites.

Dining with kids

Say 不辣 (bù là) for "not spicy." Most tourist-area restaurants have picture menus. Haidilao lets you split the pot half-and-half (鸳鸯锅) for mixed spice tolerance.

Cold weather gear

Stock up on 暖宝宝 hand warmers at any convenience store, ¥5–10 a pack — essential at the Great Wall and Tiananmen. Bring waterproof boots with grip for icy pavements.

Photography

No shooting inside certain palace halls, and no drones over any tourist site. Phone cameras are fine everywhere outdoors.

Subway

All bags pass through an X-ray at entry — keep liquids accessible. Avoid 08:00–09:30 and 17:30–19:00 with a party of six.
